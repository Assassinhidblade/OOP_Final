Our Best Category of meals

Beef

Recipes that feature beef as the main ingredient, such as burgers, steaks, and stews.

Breakfast

Breakfast is an essential meal that provides the body with the energy and nutrients it needs to function properly throughout the day.

Chicken

Recipes that feature chicken as the main ingredient, such as grilled chicken, chicken soups, and chicken salads.



Dessert

Sweet treats like cakes, pies, cookies, and ice cream, often served after a meal.

Pasta

Recipes that feature pasta as the main ingredient, such as spaghetti, macaroni and cheese, and fettuccine Alfredo.

Lamb

Recipes that feature lamb as the main ingredient, such as gyro sandwiches, lamb chops, and lamb curries.



Seafood

Recipes that feature seafood like fish, shrimp, lobster, and crab as the main ingredient.

Miscellaneous

A catch-all category for recipes that don't fit into other categories, like snacks, appetizers, or specialty dishes.

Vegetarian

Recipes that don't contain meat, fish, or poultry, but may include dairy products and eggs, and are suitable for a vegetarian diet.
